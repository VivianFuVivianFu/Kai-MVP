Your Body Knows the Way.

Somatic Compassion is not therapy, but a guided practice in safety and connection for two people.

Using gentle, intuitive touch and focused awareness, it helps calm the nervous system and release stored stress. Rooted in neuroscience, this method trusts in your body's innate ability to heal.

Our Purpose

The modern world is loud, and our bodies keep the score. We believe true healing isn’t found in thinking harder, but in feeling safer through compassionate human connection.

In workshops, we witnessed simple, guided touch create profound moments of release—the feeling of "I'm not alone." We created Kaha to share this experience with the world.

Kaha is not the healer; you and your partner are. It is simply a quiet guide for your journey back to your own inner wisdom. It’s time to log off from the noise and log in to your body.

Crucial Disclaimer:
Kaha is an AI-powered guide and is not a substitute for professional medical or psychological advice. If you are in crisis, please call or text 1737 in New Zealand to connect with a trained counsellor for free.
